What I do

International Workforce Consulting
Unsure about managing talent across borders? Concerned about compliance risks in global employment? Whether you are managing a small team or a large global workforce, I help you navigate the risks of international employment and ensure the best experience for your talent. I offer tailored solutions to confidently and compliantly manage and pay independent contractors, find the right Employer of Record, and set up legal entities in key countries.
Go-to-market Consulting
Ready to expand into new international markets? Whether you're expanding internationally to serve a key client, hunt new revenue, or impress investors, I help you develop a robust go-to-market strategy. From market research to commercial and pricing strategy. I ensure your value proposition scales across different markets and coach your sales team on cross-cultural communication to successfully close international deals.
Fractional Operations Leadership
Navigating the complexities of a new market demands strategic precision. I help businesses transform operational challenges into sustainable growth by developing tailored processes, designing efficient workflows, and advising your Product teams on what is needed to keep your clients happy. I coach teams to measure what matters, understand local context, and deliver exceptional service to your clients.
International Partnerships  
Scaling internationally? Your network is your greatest asset! I help businesses identify, negotiate, and manage optimal cross-border collaboration - whether you need local market experts to channel your solutions, service delivery partners, or consortium arrangements for funding opportunities. My approach combines deep cultural contextualizing with strategic negotiation to create win-win partnerships that drive your international growth.
